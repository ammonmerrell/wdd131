Civilizations and Leaders

Introduction to Leaders

Each leader has a unique ability that helps some aspect of the game. You continue to play as the same leader for all three "eras" or sections of the game, meaning you will keep the ability throughout the game.

Introduction to Civilizations

Civilizations have a unique ability, two unique buildings/unique inprovement, associated wonder, millitary unit, and civillian unit. You change civilizations (civ) every era, meaning you change the related bonuses three times over a game.

Which one to choose?

With so many options it can be hard to choose. Luckly, there is a guide that talks about that.
