Buildings

Antiquity Age

Culture buildings: Monument, Amphitheater. Happiness buildings: Alter, Villa, Arena. Science buildings: Library, Academy. Production buildings: Blacksmith. Millitary buildings: Barracks. Food buildings: Garden, Bath(can only be placed on river)(extra growth in city). Gold buildings: Market, Lighthouse(can only be placed in water), Ancient Bridge(can only be built in navigable rivers).

Exploration Age

Culture buildings: Kiln, Pavilion. Happiness buildings: Temple Inn(acts as food building), Menagerie. Science buildings: Observatory, University. Production buildings: Dungeon. Millitary buildings: Armaror, Shipyard(can only be build in water). Food buildings: Wharf(can only be built in water), Hospital(extra growth in city). Gold buildings: Bazaar, Guildhall, Bank, medieval Bridge(can only be built in navigable rivers).

Modern Age

Culture buildings: Museum, Opera House. Happiness buildings: City Park, Department Store, Radio Station. Science buildings Schoolhouse, Laboratory. Production buildings: Factory. Millitary buildings: Millitary Academy. Food buildings: Tenement, Cannery(extra growth in city). Gold buildings: Stock Exchange, Port(can only be built in water), Railyard, Modern Bridge(can only be built in navigable rivers).
